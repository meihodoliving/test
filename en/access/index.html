Directions & access

Guide for visiting Meihodo.

Nagakusa and Ichinokawa area, Aso City, Kumamoto Prefecture. Reach us by national and prefectural roads if driving by car. If you take the train, please contact us about travel from the nearest station.

Meihodo is located in a peaceful setting surrounded by the rich natural beauty of Aso.

You can reach us either by car or by train.

Pick-up service from the nearest station is also available, so please feel free to contact us.
