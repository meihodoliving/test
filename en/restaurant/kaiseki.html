The flow of kaiseki

Hassun

Seasonal appetizers from land and sea—a first taste of Aso’s gifts.

Wanmono

A fragrant soup built on dashi and local spring water.

Tsukuri

Fresh sashimi from Amakusa, prepared with care by our chefs.

Yakimono

Grilled specialties showcasing Aso ingredients and the aroma of charcoal.

Nimono

Simmered dishes centered on seasonal vegetables, rooted in tradition.

Dessert

Seasonal fruit and wagashi—a refined close to your meal.
