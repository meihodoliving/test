Step-by-step directions

From Ichino-kawa Station

  1. Exit the station and continue straight on the road ahead
  2. Turn right at the intersection approx. 200 m ahead
  3. Continue approx. 300 m; Ryotei Meihodo is on your left
  4. Approx. 15 minutes on foot

From Aso Station

  1. Exit the station and continue straight on the road ahead
  2. Turn left at the intersection approx. 500 m ahead
  3. Continue approx. 400 m; Ryotei Meihodo is on your right
  4. Approx. 20 minutes on foot

By car

  1. Take National Route 57 toward Aso
  2. Enter Aso city center
  3. Follow signs for the Nagakusa area and turn right
  4. Ryotei Meihodo is approx. 100 m ahead
