Buyout terms

Minimum party size

Each room requires at least half of its stated capacity.

Duration

Buyouts follow regular service hours; extensions incur additional fees.

Fees

Beyond the room charge, we add ¥1,000 per guest for exclusive use.

Booking lead time

Please reserve buyouts at least one week in advance.

Audio & special touches

Live music

Small performances may be possible; instruments may be brought in—ask when you book.

Photography

Photos and video are welcome in private rooms.
