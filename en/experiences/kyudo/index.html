弓道 Kyudo Experience

Through Japan’s classical art of the bow, cultivate focus and stillness of mind.

Kyudo

In this session you learn Japan’s traditional way of the bow step by step. Instructors guide posture and shooting form so beginners can relax and progress. Build concentration and quiet awareness while connecting with classical budo culture.

What makes this kyudo session special

Classical kyudo as a path to harmony of mind and body

Sharper focus

The attention you bring to each draw carries into everyday life as quiet mental discipline.

Harmony with nature

Practicing amid Aso’s wide landscapes invites a felt sense of alignment with the natural world.

Etiquette and form

Learn the courtesy and ritual that anchor kyudo—an entry point to Japan’s spiritual culture.
