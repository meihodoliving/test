Samurai experience

Step into programs that bring the spirit and discipline of the samurai to life.

Samurai experience program

Inspired by Edo-period samurai training—about two and a half hours of kenjutsu, tea ceremony, kyudo, taiko, and more, from tameshigiri to the closing ceremony.

Duration: 2.5 hours
From: ¥35,000

Martial arts

Train mind and body through Japan’s classical martial ways

Kyudo

Experience traditional kyudo in a full-scale dojo. Cultivate focus, calm, and balance of mind and body.

Duration: 2 hours
From: ¥15,000

Tameshigiri

A tameshigiri session with a real Japanese sword on rolled tatami. Learn posture and how to use power, and feel the weight, edge, and beauty of each cut.

Duration: 1.5 hours
From: ¥15,000

Karate

Learn Okinawa karate fundamentals and spirit. Through kata, build strength, focus, and a disciplined mind.

Duration: 1.5 hours
From: ¥15,000

Kendo

Train etiquette and spirit through kendo. Practice with the shinai, from footwork and basics to light, controlled partner drills.

Duration: 2 hours
From: ¥15,000

Cultural experiences

Deepen your appreciation of Japanese tradition and aesthetics

Tea ceremony

Learn the spirit and etiquette of chanoyu. In stillness, find calm and the meaning of ichigo ichie.

Duration: 1 hour
From: ¥10,000

Taiko

Feel the powerful rhythm of wadaiko. From basic strikes to short phrases, enjoy the energy of the drum.

Duration: 1.5 hours
From: ¥12,000

Kado

Through ikebana, meet seasonal blooms and learn Japanese aesthetics. A quiet moment to center the heart.

Duration: 1.5 hours
From: ¥22,500

Bonseki

A traditional art of sand-and-stone miniature landscapes. Quiet making helps steady body and mind.

Duration: 1.5 hours
From: ¥12,000

Training experiences

Experiences designed to center body and mind

Takigyo

Stand beneath a waterfall to steady breath and focus. Refresh in Aso’s nature through a quiet, invigorating practice.

Duration: 1.5 hours
From: ¥5,000
