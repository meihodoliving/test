Hinokinoma

The scent of natural hinoki cypress and exquisite grain—a distinctive retreat

Wrapped in the fragrance of hinoki

Hinokinoma is crafted with fine natural hinoki wood. Its aroma and rich grain create a restorative space where you can truly relax.

An indoor hinoki bath and engawa veranda invite you to feel Aso’s nature throughout your stay.
