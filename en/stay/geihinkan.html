About Geihinkan

Geihinkan is refined accommodation designed to welcome special guests in style. Surrounded by the nature of Aso, it features an open-air bath where you can savor unhurried, indulgent moments.

From the deck overlooking lush gardens, enjoy the changing seasons—a stay that feels truly woven into the landscape.
