In Aso, nature breathes,
flavors awaken the senses,
experiences carry you beyond the everyday,
a place where the spirit of Japan endures.

At the foot of majestic Mt. Aso, Meihodo is a cultural retreat where tradition and nature exist in harmony.

Across 56,000 square meters of Japanese architecture, immerse yourself in experiences that refine both mind and spirit—
from kyūdō, tameshigiri, and karate to chadō, kadō, bonseki, and the classical arts.

Here, surrounded by nature and tradition, leave the everyday behind and restore body and mind.

In Aso, discover the spirit of the samurai.

Surrounded by Aso’s wilderness, Meihodo offers a range of cultural and martial arts experiences.
Train in full gear with our samurai immersion course, loved by families and couples alike.
