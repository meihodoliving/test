Shoot locations & gallery

Film across a spacious Japanese cultural estate

Meihodo welcomes cosplay and other photography through facility rentals across our grounds. Our coordinator is a former cosplayer and studio photographer—we listen to each group’s needs so you can relax and focus on your shoot.

SPACE

Across our extensive grounds you’ll find many buildings suited to photography—a dojo, kyudo range, taiko plaza, tea room, jujitsu hall, waterfall area, drum tower, Sanmaidō hall, forest paths, and kamado cottages. Shoot outdoors or indoors for a look unlike a typical studio or urban location.
Note: Changing rooms are available. The room assigned depends on group size and activity bookings.

Using the venue

  • First-time visitors must present photo ID (driver’s license, health insurance card, student ID, etc.).
  • Fees are paid in advance at the front desk at check-in.
  • You may enter the grounds up to 15 minutes before your booked start time.
  • Your rental period includes changing time—it does not end when shooting stops; please plan accordingly.
  • Overtime exit is charged ¥1,500 per person.
  • Photography bookings are limited to one group per day.
  • Late cancellations may incur fees under our cancellation policy.
