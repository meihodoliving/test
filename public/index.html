Welcome to MEIHODO

Where tradition meets tranquility

A Journey Through Time

Nestled in the heart of Kumamoto Prefecture, our resort offers an immersive experience into Japan's rich cultural heritage. Surrounded by the majestic Aso volcanic caldera, guests can experience traditional Japanese hospitality while enjoying modern comfort.

Our resort features authentic architecture, traditional hot springs (onsen), and carefully curated cultural experiences that connect you with the essence of Japanese culture.
